  <p>The COVID-19 pandemic, caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus, highlighted the urgent need to educate the population about health, prevention, and safety measures. Communicating this information to children, however, presents unique challenges due to the complexity and sensitivity of the topic.</p>
  <p>TATUDOBEM was created to address this gap by delivering reliable and age-appropriate information about COVID-19 to children up to 13 years old. The project adopts a playful, intuitive, and engaging approach, making learning accessible, non-threatening, and meaningful for young users.</p>
  <h3>üéØ GOALS</h3>
  <ul>

    <li><b>Raise awareness</b> about the risks of the disease in a way that is engaging, non-alarming, and easy to understand.</li>
    <li><b>Encourage preventive behavior</b> by teaching simple and practical protection methods through interactive content.</li>
    <li><b>Promote self-directed learning</b>, allowing children to explore and learn at their own pace.</li>
    <li><b>Enhance engagement through gamification</b>, using playful activities to make learning feel fun and rewarding.</li>
    <li><b>Motivate knowledge acquisition</b> by combining education and entertainment, improving retention and curiosity.</li>
    <li><b>Create a safe and inclusive digital experience</b> that supports learning, exploration, and confidence-building for young users.</li>
  </ul>
